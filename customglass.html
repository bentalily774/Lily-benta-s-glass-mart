Custom Glass & Fabrication

From precision CNC cutting to high-tech switchable privacy glass, we bring your unique designs to life.

1. Precision Cutting

Product Type Customization Options Common Applications
Bespoke Shapes CNC-cut circles, ovals, triangles, or "notched" glass for corners. Custom table covers, odd-shaped windows, DIY furniture.
Hole Drilling Pre-drilled holes for handles, hinges, or electrical outlets. Glass railings, shower doors, and standoff signage.

2. Edge & Surface Work

Product Type Customization Options Common Applications
Specialized Edging Choice of Flat Polish, Beveled (slanted), Ogee (S-curve), or Seamed (sanded). Luxury dining tables, mirrors, and high-end shelving.
Sandblasting Permanent etching of logos, patterns, or full privacy frosting. Commercial office doors, branded partitions, signage.

3. Architectural Custom

Product Type Customization Options Common Applications
Back-Painted Glass Glass painted on one side in any custom RAL or Pantone color. Kitchen backsplashes, colorful whiteboards, wall cladding.
Bent / Curved Glass Glass heated and cooled over a mold to create a permanent curve. Corner display cases, curved windows, architectural railings.

4. High-Tech / Smart

Product Type Customization Options Common Applications
Switchable Privacy Laminated glass with a PDLC film that turns opaque at a flick. Executive boardrooms, high-end residential bathrooms.
One-Way Mirror Partial silvering that reflects on one side while transparent on other. Security observation rooms, "hidden" TV mirror installs.
